About Sulur

Sulur is a suburb of Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu, known for its growing residential pockets, educational institutions, local industry, and access to the Coimbatore International Airport. The area includes Sulur Lake and is connected by the Coimbatore–Trichy highway.
